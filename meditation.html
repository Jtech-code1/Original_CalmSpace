How to Practice Box Breathing

1. Inhale slowly through your nose for 4 seconds, filling your lungs with air.

2. Hold your breath gently for 4 seconds.

3. Exhale slowly through your mouth for 4 seconds, emptying your lungs.

4. Rest for 4 seconds before beginning the next cycle.

Benefits: Box breathing helps reduce stress, improve focus, and calm your nervous system. Just 5 minutes daily can make a difference.
