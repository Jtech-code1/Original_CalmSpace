Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

1

What is CalmSpace?

CalmSpace is a mental wellness app designed to help you manage stress, improve focus, and build healthier habits through guided exercises, mindfulness tools, and expert resources.

2

Is my data safe on CalmSpace?

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All personal information is encrypted and securely stored. We never share your data without your consent.

3

Do I need to pay to use CalmSpace?

CalmSpace offers both free features and premium plans. Free users can access basic exercises and resources, while premium members unlock full programs, personalized guidance, and advanced tracking.

4

Can I use CalmSpace without internet access?

Yes, many exercises and resources can be downloaded for offline use so you can take CalmSpace with you anywhere.
