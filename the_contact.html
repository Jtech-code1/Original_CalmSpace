Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

1

What is CalmSpace?

CalmSpace is a mental wellness app designed to help you manage stress, improve focus, and build healthier habits through guided exercises, mindfulness tools,
and expert resources.

2

Is my data safe on CalmSpace?

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All personal information is encrypted and securely stored. We never share your data without your consent.

3

Do I need to pay to use CalmSpace?

CalmSpace offers both free features and premium plans. Free users can access basic exercises and resources, while premium members
unlock full programs, personalized guidance, and advanced tracking.

4

Can I use CalmSpace without internet access?

Yes, many exercises and resources can be downloaded for offline use so you can take CalmSpace with you anywhere.

5

Who creates the exercises and content on CalmSpace?

Our content is designed by licensed therapists, psychologists, and wellness coaches to ensure that you get safe, effective, and research-backed guidance.

6

Can CalmSpace replace therapy or medical treatment?

No. CalmSpace is a support tool, not a substitute for professional therapy or medical care. If you are in crisis or need urgent help, please reach out to a qualified
healthcare provider.

7

How can I contact support if I have an issue?

You can reach our support team anytime through the “Contact Us” page in the app or by emailing support@calmspace.com.
