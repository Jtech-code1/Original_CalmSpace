How to Practice

1. Inhale Slowly your nose for 4 seconds, feeling your lungs fill with air.

2. Hold your breath for 4 seconds.

3. Exhale Slowly through your mouth for 4 seconds, completely emptying your lungs.

4. Rest for 4 seconds before beginning the next cycle.

Benefits: Box breathing helps reduce stress, improve focus, and calm your
nervous system. Practice regularly for best results.
