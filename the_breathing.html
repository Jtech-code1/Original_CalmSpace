Basic Deep Breathing

1. Sit Comfortably with your back straight and shoulders relaxed.

2. Inhale slowly through your nose for 4 seconds, filling your belly with air.

3. Hold your breath gently for 2 seconds.

4. Exhale slowly through your mouth for 6 seconds, releasing all tension.

5. Repeat this cycle for 5–10 minutes.

Benefits: Deep breathing calms your nervous system, lowers blood pressure, and promotes relaxation.

Other Breathing Techniques

🌬️ Box Breathing

Inhale for 4 seconds → Hold 4 seconds → Exhale 4 seconds → Hold 4 seconds.

Benefit: Improves focus, reduces anxiety, and resets your stress response.

🌿 Alternate Nostril Breathing

Close your right nostril and inhale through the left → switch nostrils and exhale → repeat on the other side.

Benefit: Balances energy, increases mental clarity, and supports relaxation.

🔥 Breath of Fire

Take short, powerful exhales through the nose while keeping inhales passive. Maintain a steady rhythm.

Benefit: Energizes the body, improves lung capacity, and detoxifies.

😴 4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale for 4 seconds → Hold for 7 seconds → Exhale for 8 seconds. Repeat 4 cycles.

Benefit: Promotes deep relaxation and helps improve sleep quality.
