How to Improve Your Sleep

1. Set a routine by going to bed and waking up at the same time daily.

2. Create a sleep-friendly environment — keep your room cool, dark, and quiet.

3. Avoid screens at least 1 hour before bed to reduce blue-light stimulation.

4. Practice relaxation — read, meditate, or use breathing exercises.

5. Limit caffeine and heavy meals in the evening.

Benefits: Quality sleep restores your body, sharpens your mind, boosts mood, and strengthens immunity.

Sleep Hygiene Practices

🌙 Nighttime Routine

Dim the lights, take a warm shower, and do calming activities before bed.

Benefit: Signals your body it’s time to sleep and relax.

📵 Digital Detox

Keep phones, laptops, and TVs away from your sleeping area.

Benefit: Reduces overstimulation and improves sleep quality.

🛏️ Comfortable Sleep Space

Invest in a supportive mattress, soft pillows, and breathable sheets.

Benefit: Enhances relaxation and prevents body aches.

🌿 Relaxation Techniques

Try deep breathing, meditation, or light stretches before sleep.

Benefit: Calms your nervous system and prepares you for rest.
